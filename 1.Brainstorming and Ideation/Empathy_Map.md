# Empathy Map

## User
Loan Applicant

### Thinks
- Will my loan be approved?
- How can I improve my eligibility?

### Feels
- Worried about loan approval.
- Wants quick results.

### Says
- I need financial support.
- I want a fast loan approval process.

### Does
- Enters personal and financial details.
- Waits for prediction results.

### Pain Points
- Slow manual verification.
- Lack of transparency.

### Gains
- Instant prediction.
- Better understanding of loan eligibility.
